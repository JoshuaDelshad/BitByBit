## Project Concept
E-commerce online store selling video games (similar to GameStop).  

Features:  
- Customer info stored in online database (login/register)  
- Customers can purchase items  
- Payments with receipt generation  
- Personalized recommendations based on preferences  
- Admin controls for restocks, returns, etc.  
- Standard e-commerce functionality, specialized for video games
